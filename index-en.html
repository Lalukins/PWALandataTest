About us

Landata has been successfully operating in the market since 1994 and is one of the ten leading Russian distributors of high-quality networking, telecommunications, computer, peripheral and control equipment.

Landata is a diversified distributor with a diversified product portfolio and can offer you a wide range of equipment and integrated solutions for projects of any complexity.

A distinctive feature of Landata's work is an individual approach to the partner's project. Landata is always ready to offer you alternative technical solutions based on equipment from several manufacturers, if necessary, the company can recommend equipment that is not included in its regular range.
